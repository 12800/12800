THE PLEDGE

Children should not be separated from their families and incarcerated. All 12,800 and counting immigrant children should be free and reunited with their loved ones.

By signing this pledge I will help spread the word about children being detained by the U.S. government and raise awareness on this issue.

I want to help create change by holding lawmakers accountable for the current zero tolerance policies that are a violation of the civil rights of immigrants seeking refuge in the US.

By taking this pledge, I vow to be on the right side of history in the reunification of immigrant children with their families.
