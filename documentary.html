DOCUMENTARY

A documentary contextualizing the information and inspiration behind the 20-foot-tall milk carton tackling child-family separation at the U.S.-Mexico border.

This documentary opens up discussions with experts on the current issue and explores the realms of law, academia, psychiatry, politics, and globalization.

The approach is to expose what is happening, how is it happening, what is currently being done about it, and how we will move forward from it with solutions to try to help the issue.

The end goal of the documentary is to further unpack the issue that the milk carton art installation is tackling and present the facts that inspired it in an intelligent and approachable way to generate conversations and positive change.
