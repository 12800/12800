WHY WE'RE DOING THIS

All 12,800 children (and counting) are not where they are supposed to be. They are missing from their schools, playgrounds, homes and families; but most importantly, they are missing their childhood.

They came here with family looking for safety, instead they are in detention centers being traumatized, away from everything and everyone they know.

In 2017, there were 2,400 children. Now, the number is 12,800, and it will keep growing. Children deserve to be free to play, learn and grow. It’s up to everyone watching what’s being done to act while demanding change.

LET EVERY CHILD FEEL PROTECTED. LET EVERY CHILD FEEL AT HOME. LET EVERY CHILD BE A CHILD.
